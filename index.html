About Us

If you had five minutes on stage what would you say? What if you had 20 slides, rotating automatically after 15 seconds? Would you launch a website? Teach a hack? Talk about recent successes? Failures?

At Ignite Frisco, we not only welcome these thoughts, but encourage them. We are looking to to give talks that will inspire and teach, not have recruiting or product pitches. The Ignite STEM Fair is a family-friendly, non-commercial event, where students and adults will be exposed to a modern field of the 21st century.

This event is being hosted by the students of Lone Star High School and Memorial High School.

WE BELIEVE IN GREAT IDEAS

Traditionally, STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics. But for the Ignite STEAM fair we are going to mix it up.

The STEAM topics that our presentations will focus on are: Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. We plan to have some truly diverse presentations.

Anyone can attend an Ignite event. The Ignite Frisco STEM Fair is aimed at students from ages Kindergarten through 12th grade, they are all welcome to listen to high school students and adults talk about STEM.
